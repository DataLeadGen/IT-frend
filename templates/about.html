Our Story

Founded in 2018 in Burhanpur, Madhya Pradesh, IT FRNDLeads began with a simple mission: to provide businesses with high-quality, reliable data for their B2B marketing and sales efforts.

What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted data partner for IT and marketing professionals across India and beyond. Our journey has been defined by our commitment to accuracy, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

As we've grown, we've continuously refined our data collection and verification processes, invested in advanced technologies, and expanded our team of data specialists to ensure we deliver the best possible service to our clients.

Our Mission
